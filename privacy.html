privacy policy

last updated: april 2025

frgrnt is a uk-based scent branding agency. we are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the uk general data protection regulation (uk gdpr) and the data protection act 2018.

1. what information we collect

we may collect your name, email address, and phone number, but only when you provide it to us directly (e.g., by email or through a project inquiry).

2. how we use your data

your information is used solely to respond to your enquiries, manage client work, and communicate with you about our services.

we do not use your data for profiling or advertising, and we never sell or share it with third parties.

3. data storage

your data is stored securely and is only retained for as long as necessary. once no longer needed, it is deleted securely.

4. cookies & tracking

this website does not use cookies, analytics tools, or any tracking technologies.

5. your rights

you have the right to access your data, request correction or deletion, and object to or restrict processing.

you can exercise these rights at any time by contacting us at info@frgrnt.com.

6. hosting

this website is hosted on cloudflare pages. cloudflare does not collect visitor data or place cookies on our behalf.

if you have any questions or concerns about your privacy or this policy, please contact:
