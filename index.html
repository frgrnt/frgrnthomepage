scent as identity

about

we infuse brand identity into scent, deepening the connection between people and place — and helping brands shape how they’re remembered.

scent branding, custom fragrance creation, and curated programs — for retail, hospitality, exhibitions, and creative spaces.

from concept to composition, we guide brands through the creation of their olfactive signature.

services

scent creation, HVAC or standalone installation, on-demand maintenance/cleaning and bespoke scent offerings.

approach

trusted by brands who see scent as part of the experience—residential developments, health & wellness, hotels, retail stores and commercial units.

from ambient scenting for wellness spaces to signature fragrances for branded environments, each project is shaped by mood, material, and identity.

our work is subtle but immersive—built to be felt, not forced.

commitment

we work with fragrance partners who share our values—focused on clean formulations, low-impact diffusion, and recyclable materials wherever possible.

we’re committed to practices that support:
— low-emission scent delivery systems
— cruelty-free, phthalate-free fragrance oils
— conscious sourcing and supply models
